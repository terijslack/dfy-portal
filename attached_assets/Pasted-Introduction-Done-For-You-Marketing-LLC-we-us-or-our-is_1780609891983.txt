By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We use the information we collect to:

* Provide, operate, and improve our marketing services

Text Message (SMS) Communications
Done For You Marketing LLC sends customer support and service-related messages to users who submit inquiries through the website dfymarketinggroup.com chat widget. Messages may include responses to support requests, ticket updates, appointment coordination, or follow-up communications related to an existing inquiry. Messaging is limited to customer care purposes and does not include promotional content.

To opt out, reply STOP to any message. To re-subscribe, reply START. For help, reply HELP or email hello@dfymarketinggroup.com. Message and data rates may apply. We do not share your phone number or SMS opt-in data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Sharing Your Information
